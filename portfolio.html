About Me

I am a web designer and front-end developer. I started my journey with coding by learning Java in 2014 and taking a few classes on C++. Since then I have been brushing up on my skills and found my way into web development in the beginning of 2017. My passion is in coding and hope to find innovative ways to make websites more fun and efficient. I am looking to learn and obtain new skills by working in an enviroment where I can develop or maintain websites.
